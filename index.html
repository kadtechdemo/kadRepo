About Us

Since 1999 Kadtech is helping clients

KADTECH INFRAPROJECTS PVT LTD, founded in 1999, is a civil engineering company with proven track records in High-Rise Building, Civil Construction projects, Structural Fabrication and specialty Geosynthetic products in India.

KADTECH also specializes in GEOSYNTHETIC products like Woven & Non-Woven Geotextiles, Geogrids, Geostraps, PVD (Pre-Fabricated Vertical Drain), Gabions, Geo-Bag, Geo-Tube, Geo-Container, Geomembrane, Geo Composites for various applications.
